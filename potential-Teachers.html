Potential Teachers

We are receiving applications for the - school year.

The basic requirements for being an English teacher at our school include: a growing, personal relationship with Jesus Christ, Bachelors of Education (or equivalent) and to be highly proficient in all aspects of the English language. Our school gives preference to candidates from an Evangelical Protestant faith background.

If you are interested in applying for a teaching position please take the following steps:

  1. Read over the CEE Teacher Frequently Asked Questions and the CEE Teacher Pre-arrival Guide, both attached below.
  2. Fill out an application and send the completed forms to teach@ceehonduras.org.
  3. Send in your reference forms, which include 2 professional reference letters and 1 pastoral reference letter.
  4. Obtain and send an electronic copy of your passport, university degree/diploma, university transcript, birth certificate, current resume and police record. All of these documents are required to work in Honduras and at CEE.
  5. Have an interview with the English Program Coordinator.

If you have any further questions please e-mail us at teach@ceehonduras.org.
